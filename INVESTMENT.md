## Company Overview (For Investors)

**Company:** ONOX Systems
**Domain:** onoxsystems.com
**Location:** Vitoria-Gasteiz, Euskadi, Spain
**Stage:** Pre-seed (building)
**Model:** B2B SaaS + API licensing + white-label
**Technology:** WebGPU 3D rendering + AI agent orchestration

**One-liner:**
ONOX Systems is the spatial intelligence layer for the autonomous web — combining real-time 3D interfaces with AI agent infrastructure, deployed from the Basque Country.

---

## Problem We Solve

1. **3D on the web is hard** — Most companies can't afford custom 3D experiences. Our viewer makes it plug-and-play.
2. **AI agents lack operational structure** — Teams don't know how to integrate AI into their workflow. ONIX provides a proven model.
3. **Deployment complexity kills velocity** — Our stack (Vercel + Supabase + Tailscale) reduces time-to-production dramatically.
