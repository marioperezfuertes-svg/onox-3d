## Why Patents Matter for ONOX

ONOX Systems operates at the intersection of:
- Real-time 3D spatial rendering in the browser (WebGPU + React Three Fiber)
- AI agent orchestration with persistent memory (ONIX)
- Autonomous deployment and self-updating software systems
- Spatial UI paradigms for non-technical users

These intersections create protectable IP surfaces. Even small patent filings increase company valuation, deter copying, and open licensing revenue channels.
